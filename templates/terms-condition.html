Terms and condition :

ACCESS TO ROOM: Check-In time is 16:00. Check-out time is 11:00. Early check-outs are permitted at no charge provided notice is given before or at check-in. Same day early check-outs will results in a one night's penalty charge equal to the 1st night's room & taxes. PARKING: Secured underground or above ground parking is available for $12 per night, per car. ROOM RATES & FAMILY PLAN: All room rates are based on single or double occupancy. Children 15 years of age and younger stay for free when sharing room with parents and using existing bedding. A surcharge of $25.00 for additional guests 16 years of age or older will apply to the room rate. PETS ARE PEOPLE TOO: Fee is 25.00 CAD per pet per night and includes a comfy pet bed, locally made organic treats and a drinking bowl which you can take home with you after your stay. Pets must always be accompanied and be no more than 25kgs in weight. The Hotel allows a maximum of 2 pets per room. SMOKING POLICY: We are proud to provide a 100% non-smoking environment to all our guests. Smoking in any guestroom or in the Hotel's public space will result in a $250.00 charge applied to your room account. Smoking is permitted at designated areas outside the hotel only. This is why we have become an EcoFund Verified business as of March 1st, 2021. As part of this program, we invite our guests to join in making a positive change. Each night you will stay with us, a $2.00 EcoFee will be applied to support large carbon reduction projects and help reduce our carbon footprint.
